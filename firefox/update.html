Tahir is updated! - Version 1.0.4

As salamu alaykum wa rahmatullah,

Below are the latest improvements for Tahir. We pray they are of benefit. If you like Tahir, please share with others (shareable links: Twitter, Facebook). You will get reward for the haram images prevented through your shares, iA. For support or comments, send us a message at dev@fushatech.com. To contribute to the code, see our github.

May Allah give us all tawfiq to protect our eyes from the impermissible.

Changelog:

  • Tahir is now available on Firefox. (JAK to brother @sh4hids)
  • Alt+K now unblurs & reblurs an image. After unbluring an image, press Alt+K again to reblur it.
  • Several technical improvements.
